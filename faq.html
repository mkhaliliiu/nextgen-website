Home FAQ

General

What services does NexGen Dubai offer?
NexGen Dubai specializes in three core areas: Media Solutions (photography, videography, social media strategy, digital marketing, brand storytelling, content creation), Educational Partnerships (connecting students with global universities, career counseling, academic collaborations), and Event Management (corporate events, educational fairs, private celebrations). We offer fully integrated, end-to-end solutions across all three pillars.
How can I contact NexGen for inquiries?
You can reach us through our Contact Us page, where you can fill out an inquiry form, schedule a meeting, or use our email at info@nexgendubai.ae. You can also connect with us via WhatsApp or through any of our social media channels. Our team typically responds within 24 hours on business days.
Can I schedule a meeting online?
Absolutely! You can request a consultation through our Contact page. Simply fill in your details and preferred time, and a member of our team will confirm your meeting slot within 24 hours. We offer both in-person meetings at our Meydan Grandstand office and virtual consultations via video call.

Media & PR

What types of media services do you provide?
We offer a comprehensive suite of media services including: professional photography and videography, social media management and strategy, digital marketing campaigns (PPC, SEO, email), brand storytelling, content creation (blogs, reels, branded posts), post-production editing, graphic design, website design and development, PR and press releases, and influencer marketing. All services are tailored to your brand's specific goals and audience.
Can NexGen handle large-scale media campaigns?
Absolutely! Our team is fully equipped to manage campaigns of any size — from targeted single-channel activations to full multi-platform brand campaigns across the UAE and internationally. We have successfully delivered large-scale campaigns for corporate clients, educational institutions, and event organizers. We provide detailed reporting and analytics throughout, so you always know the impact of every campaign.
How do I request a quote for media services?
Simply visit our Request a Quote page and fill in details about your project — the services you need, your timeline, budget range, and project goals. Our creative team will review your brief and respond with a tailored proposal within 24–48 hours. There is no commitment required to receive a quote.

Education

What kind of educational partnerships does NexGen facilitate?
We connect world-class universities and institutions with opportunities in the UAE and globally. This includes university representation partnerships, student recruitment collaborations, educational fair participations, career workshops and seminars, academic pathways for undergraduate and postgraduate programs, and institutional media and marketing support. We currently partner with universities across Canada, UK, Hungary, Malta, Netherlands, Malaysia, Albania, and Guyana.
Do you offer career counseling for students?
Yes! We provide free career counseling, university application guidance, and educational pathway advisory for students looking to study abroad. Our advisors help students identify the right programs based on their interests, academic background, and career goals. We also assist with application documentation, visa guidance, scholarship information, and pre-departure preparation — completely free of charge for eligible students.
Is the education advisory service free for students?
Yes — our education advisory and study abroad consultation service is completely free for students. We are compensated directly by our partner universities, which means there is no cost to students for counseling, application support, or guidance. To get started, visit our Study Abroad page and fill in the consultation request form.

Events

What types of events does NexGen manage?
We specialise in a broad range of events including: corporate conferences and summits, annual general meetings, product launches and brand activations, university and education fairs, networking galas and awards nights, team-building and corporate retreats, hybrid and virtual events, press conferences, private celebrations (weddings, birthdays, etc.), and cultural community events. No event is too big or too small for our team.
Can NexGen handle end-to-end event planning?
Yes — we offer full end-to-end event management. This covers initial concept development and strategy, venue sourcing and booking, event design and décor, catering coordination, technical support (AV, lighting, staging), on-site staffing and guest management, media coverage (photography and videography), social media promotion, PR and press outreach, and post-event reporting. You brief us once and we handle everything.
How do I start planning an event with NexGen?
Getting started is simple. Visit our Plan Your Event page, fill in your event brief — including event type, date, estimated attendance, budget range, and your vision — and submit. Our events team will review your brief and contact you within 24 hours with a customised proposal. Alternatively, you can email us directly at info@nexgendubai.ae.

Partnerships

Who can partner with NexGen?
We collaborate with a wide range of partners including businesses (SMEs and corporates), universities and educational institutions, media houses and production companies, event organizers, NGOs and government bodies, and individual professionals and consultants. We operate across the UAE and maintain an international network spanning over 12 countries. If you have a project or idea, we'd love to explore how we can work together.
How can I partner with NexGen?
To explore a partnership, visit our Join Our Educational Network page or our Contact page and fill in the relevant form. Clearly describe the nature of the collaboration you have in mind and our partnership team will reach out within 48 hours to discuss next steps. We welcome partnerships of all shapes and sizes.
Are there exclusive benefits for partnering institutions and businesses?
Yes — NexGen provides exclusive benefits for all established partners including priority access to our services and resources, customized strategies tailored to your organization's goals, a dedicated point of contact from our team, inclusion in our event promotions and PR campaigns, co-branding opportunities, and access to our regional network. Long-term partners also benefit from preferential pricing on all services.
