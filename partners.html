Algoma University
Ontario, Canada
Business Computer Science Sciences Social Work Humanities
Apply Now

Algoma University is a public university in Ontario, Canada, with campuses in Sault Ste. Marie, Brampton, and Timmins. Established in 1965 and gaining independence in 2008, it offers over 30 academic programs across liberal arts, sciences, business, and technology. Known for its small class sizes and personalized learning, Algoma emphasizes cross-cultural education — particularly fostering understanding between Indigenous and non-Indigenous communities.

Ras Al Khaimah, UAE
Business Cyber Security AI Engineering British Degree

The University of West London (UWL) Ras Al Khaimah Branch Campus offers students in the UAE a unique opportunity to earn a prestigious British degree while studying in a dynamic and affordable environment. Established in 2017 within the RAKEZ Academic Zone, UWL RAK mirrors the academic excellence of its UK counterpart, providing career-focused programs in Business, Finance, Computing, and Engineering.

John von Neumann University
Kecskemét, Hungary
Engineering Computer Science Horticulture EU Degree
Apply Now

John von Neumann University, located in Kecskemét, Hungary, is a dynamic institution offering a blend of academic excellence and practical training. Named after the renowned Hungarian-American scientist John von Neumann, the university has become a significant educational, scientific, and cultural center in the Southern Great Plain Region. It offers EU-recognized degrees at highly competitive tuition fees.

Programs Offered

Faculty of Engineering & Computer Science (GAMF)

High school diploma or equivalent — 12 years of education
Satisfactory grades in relevant sciences (Maths, Physics, Chemistry)

Qadri International Educational Consultancy
Dubai, UAE
Medicine Dentistry Nursing Pharmacy Health Sciences
Apply Now

Qadri International Educational Consultancy, established in 1986, is one of the UAE's most experienced and trusted student counseling firms, headquartered in Dubai's Business Bay. With over 39 years of expertise, the consultancy has guided more than 550,000 students and facilitated the overseas education of over 70,000 individuals, maintaining a 98% success rate. They specialize in placing students in medical and health science programs across Europe.

Shargan Consulting is a professional services and university placement firm based in Tirana, Albania.
